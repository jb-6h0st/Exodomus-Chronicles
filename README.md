# 🌌 Exodomus Chronicles: Lumina Perdida

> *Recover the past. Rewrite the future.*  
> A collector-driven, story-based game powered by NFTs, on-chain identity, and AI-guided quests.

---

## 📖 About

**Lumina Perdida** is a storytelling game set in a dystopian city where memories were erased to control the people. Now, memory fragments stored as NFTs are resurfacing, and it's your role to collect, uncover, and restore them one episode at a time.

Players evolve through soulbound roles, solve metadata-based puzzles, and unlock interactive storylines all powered by the Solana blockchain.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay Highlights

- 🧩 **Mintable Memory NFTs**  
  Mint story fragments and uncover hidden metadata-based quests.

- 🔥 **Burn-to-Reveal Mechanic**  
  Fuse memory NFTs to reveal the full story and evolve your role.

- 🧠 **Soulbound Progression System**  
  Reputation is on-chain. Complete tasks to upgrade your Collector status.

- 🤖 **Narrative Bot (6h0st)**  
  An AI-powered guide issues riddles, missions, and assists your story arc.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Blockchain:** Solana  
- **NFT System:** Mint → Quest → Burn → Evolve  
- **Soulbound Roles:** On-chain progression identity  
- **Frontend:** Next.js (TBC)  
- **Agent Layer:** AI-powered NPC-style guide bot
